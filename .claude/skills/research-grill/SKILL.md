# Research Grill

Interview the researcher until the claim, design, or method is precise enough
to act on.

## Process

1. Read `CONTEXT.md` and any relevant decision records.
2. Identify the thing being stress-tested: research question, method, dataset,
   model, statistical design, interpretation, or paper argument.
3. Ask one question at a time. For each question, include your recommended
   answer or the tradeoff you see.
4. Resolve dependencies in order:
   - scientific claim
   - target population or spatial/temporal scope
   - data fitness
   - method assumptions
   - validation strategy
   - expected output
   - failure modes
5. When a durable decision emerges, offer to record it in
   `context/decisions/`.

## Output

Finish with:

- sharpened research question or claim
- agreed assumptions
- unresolved risks
- recommended next action
